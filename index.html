Our Mission

The Edison Rotary Club is an organization that provides a platform for passionate community members and students of Edison to give back to their community. Our club is led by a group of dedicated community members and staff who are dedicated to promoting volunteerism, student success, and academic excellence.

Our club is committed to playing our role in making the world a better place, one initiative at a time. We provide a supportive and inclusive environment for students, staff, and our community members to thrive and do their part in keeping Edison great!
